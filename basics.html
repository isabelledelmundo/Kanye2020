KanUHack

Are you as frustrated as us with the world politics situation? Maybe you want to see some change happening in the near future, aka 2020?

Clarifai provides image recognition APIs, which we used to build a web app to block Donald Trump's face out of everything. (And replace it with Kanye2020 Propaganda!)

About KanUHack

Built in a 24-hour hackathon at Concordia University, KanUHack is a fun Google Chrome extension built to replace photos of Donald Trump, President of the United States, with those of Kanye West.
